Large Group Trip 2D1N

Explore the rich culture of agriculture in India on this 2-day tour.

Package Description

Step away from the fast-paced urban lifestyle and embrace the simplicity of nature with our Solo Agro Tourism Experience. Nestled in the heart of the countryside, this trip offers a rejuvenating escape tailored for individual travelers seeking tranquility and self-discovery. Immerse yourself in sustainable farming practices, explore rural life at your own pace, and experience eco-conscious living. Whether you are an eco-enthusiast or simply seeking a peaceful retreat in nature, this solo journey promises an unforgettable and personal connection with the land.

Itinerary

  • Day 1: Arrival and village walk, exploring local crafts, tea on mountain, horse riding
  • Day 2: Farm visit, explore traditional farming methods, Indulge in cooking activity, Camel Riding
